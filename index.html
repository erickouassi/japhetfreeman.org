Mission Statement

The mission of Japhet Freeman Institute of Public Health is to preserve, promote, and improve the health and wellbeing of populations, communities, and individuals. To fulfill this mission, we foster collaborations among public health and the health professionals in education, research, and services.

Vision Statement

We hope to foster a community in which all people achieve their full potential for health and well-being across the lifespan. We work to be trusted by the public by being a valued partner in the community, and creators of positive change.

Goal

Our goal is to promote sound health and prevent disease in the population. Our team adopts a population-based perspective on pressing health issues, through health education campaigns, screening and prevention programs, epidemiologic analysis of health problems, and consideration of implications of health policies.
